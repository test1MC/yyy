  • Clicking the shape properties toolbar button

Typing Box tab

UI controls
  • Start Text:Text that will show up at the beginning of the frame in the typing box.
  • Typed Text:Text that will automatically type in the typing box for the duration of the frame.
  • Enable Sound Effect:Plays a 'typing' sound effect as the typed text is typed.

Polyline - UI

General
  • Clicking the Polyline toolbar item on the local frame toolbar will let you draw a polyline object on a frame.
    • After clicking the object, click to add a new segment of the polyline. Each additional click will add a new segment, connected by a resize point.
    • Object is realizable by the resize points around the object.
  • Clicking and dragging the polyline will drag the polyline object to a new location.
  • No typing on polylines.

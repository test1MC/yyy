Rollover - UI

  •   Clicking the rollover caption toolbar item on the frame toolbar will let you draw a rollover caption object on a frame
    • After clicking the object, click and drag on the frame to set the object size
    • Object is realizable by the 8 resize points around the object
    • The single resize point at the pointer of the rollover caption can be used to move the pointer to a new location and/or change its length
  • Clicking and dragging the rollover caption will drag the whole object, including the pointer
  • Clicking and dragging the icon on the rollover caption (hover over the rollover caption to make this appear) will drag just the rollover caption. The pointer will remain at its current location, while the pointer tail section will get longer or shorter depending on where the rollover caption is moved to.
  • Pressing F2 will bring up a rich text window
  • Typing with the object selected will bring up a rich text window

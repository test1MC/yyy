Bubble - UI

General
  •   Clicking the Bubble toolbar item on the frame toolbar will let you draw a bubble object on a frame
    • After clicking the object, click and drag on the frame to set the object size
    • Object is realizable by the 8 resize points around the object
    • The single resize point at the pointer of the bubble can be used to move the pointer to a new location and/or change its length
  • Clicking and dragging the bubble will drag the whole object, including the pointer
  • Clicking and dragging the icon on the bubble (hover over the bubble to make this appear) will drag just the bubble. The pointer will remain at its current location, while the pointer tail section will get longer or shorter depending on where the bubble is moved to.
  • Pressing F2 will bring up a rich text window
  • Typing with the object selected will bring up a rich text window

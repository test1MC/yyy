Audio - Editing

Opening the Audio Tab:

Opening the Editing Tool:

  1. Open the Audio tab See Audio - UI
  2. Click the Edit button (a sound must be selected first)

UI - Creating and Editing Conditions:

  • Name of the sound will be shown in the title bar

  • OK: Closes dialog saving changes to the waveform
  • Cancel:Closes the dialog without saving changes to the waveform
  • Toolbar Items:
    • Copy:Copies the waveform, or the selected area
    • Cut:Cuts the selected area of the waveform, adding it to the clipboard
    • Paste:Pastes copied/cut content from the clipboard at the insertion area
    • Play/Pause: Will play the audio of the waveform.
      • Changes to Pause while sound is playing. Pressing it will pause the audio.
      • While paused, button changes to Play. Will play the audio when pressed.
      • If a portion of the waveform is selected, Play will only play that selection
    • Stop: Stops the playback of the waveform, returning the position line to the beginning
    • Insert Silence...: Opens the Insert Silence dialog.
    • Record...: Opens the Record dialog. See Audio - Recording
      • Will insert the new recording at the position line
    • Adjust Volume...: Opens the Adjust Volume dialog.
    • Zoom in: Will zoom into the waveform
      • Mouse Wheel up will also accomplish this
    • Zoom out: Will zoom out of the waveform
      • Mouse Wheel down will also accomplish this task

 

Insert Silence

  • Duration: Sets the amount of silence that will be inserted.

  • Position: sets the insertion point for the silence.
    • Current Selection: Inserts a silence over the current selection (will ignore the duration setting)
    • Playhead position: Inserts a silence at the playhead position of the duration set
    • Start of Audio: Inserts a silence at the start of the audio for the duration set
    • End of Audio: Inserts a silence at the end of the audio for the duration set
  • OK: Closes the dialog and inserts silence into the waveform, with the chosen settings
  • Cancel: Closes the dialog discarding any silence changes

Adjust Volume

  • Volume: Adjusts the volume of the waveform or the selected area of the waveform

  • Play/Pause: Will play the audio of the waveform.
    • Changes to Pause while sound is playing. Pressing it will pause the audio.
    • While paused, button changes to Play. Will play the audio when pressed.
    • If a portion of the waveform is selected, Play will only play that selection
  • Stop: Stops the playback of the waveform
  • Normalize: Boosts audio level to maximum levels without clipping
  • Dynamics: Boosts the "quiet" portions of the audio. Use ratio and threshold sliders to determine what constitutes as "quiet".
    • Threshold: Specifies the "non-quiet" portion.
    • Ratio: Specifies how much the "non-quiet" portion of the audio will be compressed.
  • OK: Closes the dialog and inserts silence into the waveform, with the chosen settings
  • Cancel: Closes the dialog discarding any silence changes

Frames Pane

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Movies/Frames

 

Description:

The Frames pane is a pane that displays all the frames of the current movie. The frames are shown in a descending order with a scroll bar that navigates to frames further down the list. The frames will appear in order that they will be played in when the movie is compiled. The user has many editing options available to them in this pane.

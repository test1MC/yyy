Record Movie - Test Cases

Use Cases

  • Verify accuracy of mouse clicks (if cursor is turned on)
  • Verify callouts at every mouse click (if option is enabled)
  • Verify accuracy of recording
  • Verify typing actions can be recorded
  • Verify Right-Click menus are recorded
  • Verify, while recording, files and folders can be opened with a Double-Click
  • Verify actions taking place outside of the recording area are not registered/recorded
  • Verify sub-menus are recorded
  • Verify the timing is accurate (ie moving through a wizard)
    • Mainly, pay attention to the frame that appears with the appropriate mouse click

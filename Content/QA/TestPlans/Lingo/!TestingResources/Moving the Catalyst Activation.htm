Moving your Catalyst Activation to a new PC

Reason

As Alchemy Catalyst  licenses are tide to the MAC addresses of the hardware of the PC you installed on, moving the application to a new PC requires to deactivate the license on the current PC and activating on the new.

 

Solution

Delete previously issued license and certificate files

Using Windows Explorer, navigate to your Alchemy Software directory in your All Users/Public folder and delete all previously issued *.CERT and *.LIC files.

Vista & Windows 7

  • C:\Users\Public\Alchemy Software\Catalyst x

Windows XP

  • C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Alchemy Software\Catalyst x

Run Deactivate.exe in the Alchemy Catalyst  folder

The first step in permanently moving your license is to deactivate your existing Alchemy Catalyst  license. To deactivate, navigate to your existing Alchemy CATALYST installation directory; the default installation path is: C:\Program Files\Alchemy Software\Catalyst x

Double click on the Deactivate.exe to launch the deactivation application.

Select the license you wish to deactivate

Select the relevant license you wish to deactivate on your machine from the list provided in the drop down menu.

The following deactivation key (89263659652550371861) should be used when requested by our deactivation application; this can be seen in the screenshot below:

Deactivation Details

Next you need to send the deactivation details to info@alchemysoftware.ie

Request a License for Alchemy Catalyst  on the new PC

Next launch Alchemy Catalyst  on the machine which you wish to move your license to and select the Activate Alchemy Catalyst option. Then click OK.

Select Step 1 and follow the instructions on screen to request a license for your new installation of Alchemy Catalyst .

Products or Versions Affected

Alchemy CATALYST 6.0  and greater

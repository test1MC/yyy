Duplicate Styles - Test Cases

17287: Duplicate Styles pane does not take into account declarations (properties) assigned to different mediums.

17297: Duplicate Styles which happen to also duplicate a table style do not appear in the Duplicate Styles pane.

17298: Duplicate Styles which happen to also duplicate a factory style sheet do not appear in the Duplicate Styles pane.

17377: In the Duplicate Styles window, paired duplicates do not highlight correctly if the Declarations for two separate duplicate sets happen to be the same.

17555: Duplicate styles' declarations are not case sensitive, however in a mixed-case situation, such duplicate styles do not appear correctly in the Duplicate Styles window or a Report.

17559: If you have two sets of duplicate styles for different tags which have the same declarations, they appear in a Duplicate Styles report as one.

17589: Normalized Declarations display mixed casing.

17674: Styles which are essentially the same, but one has a namespace, still display as Duplicate Styles.

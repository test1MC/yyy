Project Annotations

Bug Base Product Area:

Description:

This window displays all annotations in your project. You can double-click a row to open the topic with the annotation in the Analyzer interface. You can also select the row and click "Send to Flare" or "Send to Blaze" to open the topic in the appropriate application.

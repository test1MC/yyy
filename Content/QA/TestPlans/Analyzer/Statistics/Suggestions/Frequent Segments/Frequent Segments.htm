Frequent Segments

Bug Base Product Area:

  •  

 

Description:

This window displays a list of segments that occur frequently in your project. Segments are simply phrases or sentences with punctuation at the end. You can easily convert frequent segments to snippets or variables by using the "Create" button in the local toolbar as well as be prompted to apply the snippet or variable to the referenced topics. You can also use the "Search Properties" button to limit the frequent segments that are displayed.

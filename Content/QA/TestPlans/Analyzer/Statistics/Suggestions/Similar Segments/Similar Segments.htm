Similar Segments

Bug Base Product Area:

  •  

 

Description:

This window displays a list of segments in your project that are similar, but not identical, to each other. Segments are simply phrases or sentences with punctuation at the end. You may want to modify similar segments so that they become identical to each other, or even convert them to snippets. You can also use the "Search Properties" button to limit the similar segments that are displayed.

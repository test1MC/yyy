LICENSING

Our licensing model allows for distribution of single or multi-user product license keys, regulated on MadCap servers by unique machine ID or concurrent active session limits. We can generate single-app or MadPak keys and offer both a non-expiring or subscription based model. We also have the ability to add/remove products from existing licenses, and blacklist licenses.

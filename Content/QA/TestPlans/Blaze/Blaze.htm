BLAZE

Long Document Authoring for Print Publishing

MadCap Blaze™ is a powerful, XML-based content authoring application for long printed documents, such as technical manuals, policy & procedure manuals, reference guides, and more. MadCap Blaze utilizes efficient topic-based authoring and single-sourcing allowing technical writers and documentation specialists to save valuable time when creating complex print projects.

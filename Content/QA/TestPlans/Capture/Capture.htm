CAPTURE

MadCap Capture lets you easily capture anything that is displayed on your computer screen, from window panels to entire screen shots, as an editable image. Further enhance captured images by adding text call-outs, cursor arrows, special effects and more.

  • Enhance the usability and look of your online and print documentation
  • Utilize single-source images for print and web
  • Add text callouts and other objects separate from image
  • Customizable user interface elements that can be docked to different areas of the workspace
  • Enjoy tight integration with other MadCap products such as Flare, Blaze, Mimic, and MadCap Lingo
  • Auto-scroll windows capture option
  • Standardize image settings using profiles
  • Add special effects to draw user attention

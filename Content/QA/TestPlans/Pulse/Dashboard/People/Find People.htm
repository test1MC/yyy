Find People

Select this menu command to search through the list of people in Pulse.

  • New People: Shows the most recently created users.
  • Most Followed People: Shows the users with the largest combined number of followers and tags.
  • Most Active People: Shows the users with the greatest amount of activity.
  • Followed by Me
  • All People: Shows a list of all registered Pulse users.
  • Search People...: Type a query in the Search field. Then press Enter. Matches appear in the Find People page.

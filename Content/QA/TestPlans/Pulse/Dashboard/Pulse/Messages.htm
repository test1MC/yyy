Messages

All conversation history will be available in the Messages tab. 

  • New Message: Opens the New Message Dialog.

    • To: Begin typing to see user suggestions - click the name or press Enter to add it. You can add multiple names.
    • Message: Type your message here. This text input field can be re sized by dragging the lower right corner.
    • Attachment: Click Add Attachment to open the Open dialog.
  • Hide Conversation: Click the gray x to the right of the message to hide the conversation.  It will only reappear if you start another conversation with that person, or they message you again.

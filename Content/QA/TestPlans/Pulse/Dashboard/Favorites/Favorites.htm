Favorites

Bug Base Product Area:

Description:

The Favorites menu will appear if you have marked any topics as favorites in your help system. Click the word "Favorites" to open the dashboard to the Favorites page.  Hover over the tab to show a dropdown of the topics you have marked as favorites.

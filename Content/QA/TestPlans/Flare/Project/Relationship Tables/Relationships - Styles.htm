Relationships Heading Styles

Set properties on the MadCap|relationshipsHeading style to affect the headings that appear for the relationship links. Style classes of MadCap|relationshipHeading correspond to the Column Types used in your Relationship Table. SO, items which appear in the "concept" column in your Relationship Table will appear under the heading that you create with the MadCap|relationshipsHeading.concept style class. Similarly, any custom columns that are created in your Relationship Tables will need to have classes of MadCap|relationshipsHeading created in the stylesheet if you want to customize their headings.

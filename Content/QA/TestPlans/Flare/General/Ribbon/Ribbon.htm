Ribbon

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Toolbars / Menus

 

Description:

Starting with Flare Version 8 , the primary way to use the graphical interface is thought the ribbon. Additionally frequently used items on the ribbon can be added to the quick access toolbar by Right Clicking on the icon and selecting "Add to Quick Access Toolbar". Items on the Quick Access Toolbar shall have the same functionality as their counter parts that reside in the ribbon. To remove an Item from the "Quick Access Toolbar" simply right click on the item and select "Remove from Quick Access Toolbar". Items that are removed from the "Quick Access Toolbar" can be re-sent to the toolbar using the same right click method. Icons shall stay on the "Quick Access Toolbar" regardless of how many times MadCap Flare is terminated and restarted. Icons on the Quick Access Toolbar will only be removed when a User makes an explicit decision to do so.

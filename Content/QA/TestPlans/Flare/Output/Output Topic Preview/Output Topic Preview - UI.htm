Output Topic Preview- UI

Dropdown in the Local Topic Toolbar that allows you to preview your topic in the format of any of your topics. By default, it takes any conditions applied to your target and applies them in the preview.

 

In the Preview dialog, click the down arrow to the right of the Include Expression and Exclude Expression fields.

A window opens, showing the condition tags in your project.

 

Next to each condition tag, select either the Include or Exclude check box, depending on whether you want the content with the corresponding tag to be seen in the output.

As you make your selections, the area at the bottom of the window indicates which tags will be seen in the output.

Note: The topic preview feature is not available for Microsoft Word or Adobe FrameMaker formats.

Locked Elements

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Locked Elements

 

Description:

You can lock areas within topics, such as paragraphs, so that they cannot be edited. You might take advantage of this feature, for example, when you are using Flare's topic review and contributions process (see About Topic Reviews and Contributions). If you want a reviewer to see portions of a topic, but you do not want that individual to alter them, you can lock those parts. After the file is returned, you can unlock those areas of the file. See Unlocking Elements.

 

When you mark areas in a file to be locked, the background of that area turns gray. However, you can turn this background on or off, depending on whether you want to see it when working on the file.

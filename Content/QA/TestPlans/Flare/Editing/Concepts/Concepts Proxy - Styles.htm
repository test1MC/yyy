Concepts Proxy - Styles

The styles detailed below all contribute to the look of the generated concept references in place of the Concepts Proxy. Of the styles outlined here, only MadCap|conceptsProxy will allow you to create unique classes. All other styles are generated styles used by all Concept Proxies regardless of class.

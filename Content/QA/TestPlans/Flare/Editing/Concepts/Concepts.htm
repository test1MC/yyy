Concepts/Concept Proxies/Alinks

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Concepts/Concept Proxies/Alinks

 

Description:

A concept is a marker that you can insert into related topic files in order to "tie" them together with a Concept Link, or A-Link. Concepts can also be collected into a concept proxy which, in the output, will display all concepts used throughout the project and create references (links) to the topics containing those concepts.

Concepts are also used for the purposes of creating Search Filters in Web outputs, but those will be covered in the Search Filters test plan.

Snippets

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Snippets

 

Description:

Snippets are pre-set chunks of content that you can use in your project over and over. Snippets are often used for longer chunks of content that you can format just as you would any other content in your topic. In Snippets, you can also insert tables, pictures and what ever else can be included in a normal topic. You can link to a Stylesheet or upon inserting the snippet it will inherit the page Stylesheet. There are two types of snippets Text (single paragraph or portion of one) and Block (multiple paragraphs).

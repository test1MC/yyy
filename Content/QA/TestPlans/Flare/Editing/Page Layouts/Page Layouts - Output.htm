Support in PDF Outputs:

  •  Verify that frames appear correctly
    • body frames
      • columns
      • multiple body frames
      • rotated body frames
      • frame-break properties apply correctly (frame-break-after/before/inside: always/auto/avoid/inherit)
    • Header/Footer frames
    • Decoration frames
    • Image frames

      • multiple body frames*
      • rotated body frames* 40041 Page layout frame: Rotated frames are not supported in this output

    • Header/Footer frames
    • Decoration frames
    • Image frames

Page layout First does not render in a Word Output (bug 50666)
Page layout breaks do not work for word output. (bug 54875)
MS word does not support multi-columns (bug 18250)
Page layouts containing multiple body frames are not supported, text will show in the first body frame only. (bug 30400)
The empty page types are not supported in Microsoft Word output.
Rotated frames are not supported: If it has been rotated, your text will not be rotated and will fill the from top to bottom. Text will render fine if your frame is rotated 90 degrees. If your body frame is rotated at another angle (with or without columns), your text will span the farthest left and right reach of the frame.

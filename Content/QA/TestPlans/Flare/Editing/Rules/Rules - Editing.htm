Rules - Editing

Rules menu

 

 

Open up the ruler edit dialog and click 'remove ruler'

 

Test Cases

  • Pasting another ruler over the existing one should replace the selected ruler – Verify that it renders and display in the block bar correctly.
  • Pasting another element or text over a selected ruler will replace it with that data. – Verify that there is no malformed XML from this
  • Inserting another element or typing over a selected ruler will replace it with that data. – Verify that there is no malformed XML from this

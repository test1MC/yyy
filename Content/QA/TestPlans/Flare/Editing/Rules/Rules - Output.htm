Rules - Output

Support in HTML5 Outputs:

  • Verify that the HR and all of its properties are removed when conditionalized out.
  • Verify that the HR and all of its properties are unbound when conditionalized out with the "Unbind" behavior.
  • Verify that applied styles are in outputs.

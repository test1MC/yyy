Internal Text Editor - Editing

You should be able to place the insertion point anywhere in the Internal Text Editor and perform basic editing of any kind:

 

Selecting:

Select All:

Right-click anywhere in the editor and select Select All

Press CTRL-A on the keyboard

 

Select a word:

Double-click a word

 

Selecting multiple words or chunks of text:

Click at the beginning of the desired selection and drag forward to the end of the desired selection.

Click at the end of the desired selection and drag back to the beginning of the desired selection.

Place the insertion point at the beginning of the desired section and press <Shift><Right Arrow> to select one character at a time going forwards.

Place the insertion point at the end of the desired section and press <Shift><Left Arrow> to select one character at a time going backwards.

Place the insertion point at the beginning of the desired section and press <CTRL><Shift><Right Arrow> to select one word at a time going forwards.

Place the insertion point at the end of the desired section and press <CTRL><Shift><Left Arrow> to select one word at a time going backwards.

 

Cut/Copy & Paste – Paste options will only be active if there is data on the clipboard

Once a selection is made using any of the methods described above:

Right-click on the selected text, and select Cut,Copy or Paste from the right-click menu

Cut, Copy and Paste Standard Toolbar options

Cut, Copy and Paste Keyboard keys

Cut, Copy and Paste !Edit Menu options

Pasting text over an existing text selection should replace the selected text

 

Deleting

Place the insertion point before the text you want to delete and press <Delete>

Place the insertion point after the text you want to delete and press <Backspace>

Place the insertion point before the text you want to delete and press <CTRL><Delete> to delete one word at a time

Place the insertion point after the text you want to delete and press <CTRL><Backspace> to delete one word at a time

Select the text you want to delete and press <Delete>

Select the text you want to delete and press <Backspace>

Select the text you want to delete and “Cut”

Typing over selected text will replace it with the new typed text.

Drag and Drop

Dragging and dropping text inside the Internal Text Editor:

Once a selection is made using any of the methods described above:

Left- or right-click the text in the editor and drag it to its new location within the Internal Text Editor

Try to drag text out of the Internal Text Editor into the Content Explorer, Project Organizer, Frame Contents Pane, Footnotes Pane, etc. (You should not be able to)

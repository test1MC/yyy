Drop-Down Text

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Links: Exp/Drop/Togglers

 

Description:

A feature that lets you "scrunch up" content in your topic. The content is expanded (and therefore displayed) when the end user clicks a link. For example, let's say you have a topic that seems to be getting quite lengthy. So you decide to condense the portion of the topic that contains step-by-step procedures. When users open the topic in the output, they will see a link (also called a "drop-down hotspot"). This hotspot is the first paragraph (or a section of the first paragraph) that you condensed. When users click the hotspot, the hidden content is displayed below the hotspot. When users click the hotspot again, the content becomes hidden once more.

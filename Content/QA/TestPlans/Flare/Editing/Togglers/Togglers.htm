Togglers

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Links: Exp/Drop/Togglers

 

Description:

Togglers let you "toggle" between hiding and showing a chunk of named content (e.g., paragraph, image, table, list, div). When users open the topic in the output, they will see a link (also called a "toggler hotspot"). This hotspot can be any text located anywhere in the topic. When users click the hotspot, the hidden content is displayed. When users click the hotspot again, the content becomes hidden once more.

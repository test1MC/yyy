Images - Image Maps

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Images/Multimedia

 

Description:

After you insert a picture into a topic, you can create multiple hyperlinks for specific sections of the image. This is called an "image map." Supported in all online outputs and in PDF (See Image Maps - Output).

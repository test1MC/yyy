Redacted Text

Bug Base Product Area:

  • Redacted Text

 

Description:

Text that is "blacked out" in PDF or XPS output. This is much more efficient than attempting to black out text or images after the document has been printed. What is meant by "blacked out"? Redaction occurs when content is permanently eliminated from a printed or electronic document. In place of that content, end users will see black rectangles that indicate where the original content was found. When you use this feature, the text in the generated PDF or XPS output is truly gone. There is no way a user can peek into the PDF or XPS output and find it.

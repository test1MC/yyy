Context menu

 

 

 

 

 

Code

No Class:

<MadCap:footnote>
<p>This is the sample footnote</p>
</MadCap:footnote>

 

With Class:

<MadCap:footnote Class =”Styles”>
<p>This is the sample footnote</p>
</MadCap:footnote>

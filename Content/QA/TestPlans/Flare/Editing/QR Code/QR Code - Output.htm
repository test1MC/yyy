Support in HTML5 Outputs:

  • Verify that the QR Code and all of its properties are removed when conditionalized out.
  • Verify that the QR Code and all of its properties are unbound when conditionalized out with the "Unbind" behavior.
  • Verify that applied styles are in outputs.
  • Test lots of different characters including foreign characters and make sure they all render correctly and output in all outputs.

  • Verify QR codes appear correctly in Print/Web outputs and that they are able to be read by QR code readers and what is read is correct.

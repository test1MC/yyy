Equation menu

 

General Editing

 

Test Cases

  • Pasting another equation over the existing one should replace the selected equation – Verify that it renders and display in the block bar correctly.
  • Pasting another element or text over a selected equation will replace it with that data. – Verify that there is no malformed XML from this
  • Inserting another element or typing over a selected equation will replace it with that data. – Verify that there is no malformed XML from this
  • Test lots of different characters including foreign characters and make sure they all render correctly

 

Misc

Code

No Class:

<MadCap:equation xmlns:dsi="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML">

<math>

<mrow>

<mn>2</mn>

<mo>+</mo>

<mn>2</mn>

</mrow>

</math>

</MadCap:equation>

 

With Class:

<MadCap:equation xmlns:dsi="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" class="equation">

<math>

<mrow>

<mn>2</mn>

<mo>+</mo>

<mn>2</mn>

</mrow>

</math>

</MadCap:equation>

Explanation of product license keys

License keys for all MadCap products are designed to work for one product only. The product to which a license key belongs is easily determined by observing the first two characters in the key. Below is a list of all MadCap products and any license keys they have ever used (both previous and current). Unreleased products are included for archival purposes.

Explanation of evaluation keys

Evaluation keys (or "training keys") are generally given to customers who have not purchased a product, but need a key to bypass the trial limitations. They can also be given to customers for the purpose of training. Essentially, they allow a customer to use the products as if they had a regular license key. However, all evaluation keys expire after so many days.

To acquire an Evaluation Key, contact Frank.

 

Evaluation Key

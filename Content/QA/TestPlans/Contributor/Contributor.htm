Contributor

Contributor is an XML-based professional content authoring software application that allows reviewers and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to review and contribute content to a Flare project. Refer to the Contributor - Testing.htm document for an overview of Contributor, what the Contributor workflow is, and how to test Contributor and the Contributor Workflow. If you see old bugs referring to X-Edit, treat them as Contributor bugs. X-Edit is now Contributor.
